About Danny DeVito

Danny DeVito, born November 17, 1944, is an American actor, comedian, and filmmaker. Known for his unique appearance, charisma, and versatile acting talent, DeVito has captivated audiences for decades. From his early days on the TV show "Taxi" to his hilarious and quirky roles in films like "Twins," "Matilda," and "It's Always Sunny in Philadelphia," Danny DeVito has become a beloved figure in Hollywood and pop culture.

With an acting career spanning over 50 years, DeVito has also made a mark as a director and producer, bringing countless memorable films and TV shows to life. His humor, larger-than-life personality, and unmistakable voice continue to charm audiences around the globe.
